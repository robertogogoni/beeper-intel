## What "Coming Soon" Actually Means

Based on batuhan's track record in the Dev Community:

| Promise | Timeframe Stated | Actual Delivery |

| "Fix in tomorrow's release" | 1 day | Usually accurate |
| "This week" | 3-5 days | Often slips to next week |
| "Next week" | 5-10 days | Generally accurate |
| "Coming soon" | 2-4 weeks | Variable |
| "On the roadmap" | Unknown | Could be months |
| "Not soon" | 3+ months | Honest assessment |
| "No ETA" | Unknown | Genuinely undetermined |
